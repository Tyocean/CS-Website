About Us

Helping out people in need is great! I have spent countless hours helping out at Boca Helping Hands.

As I first arrived at the soup kitchen, I was very excited to help out people in need. We opened the door and people started pouring in. The only thing I had to do was hand them the food. I talked to them while they ate, and they were pretty nice people. After everyone had eaten, we started cleaning up and I helped out. I left after we finished. It felt good to help out people in need.

Cameron Tyriver - Saint Andrew's School Website
